Mission Statement

Freeganese is Seattle's one and only pay if you can, completely vegan, Vietnamese inspired cuisine. Come with your families and enjoy the fine atmosphere, meet some new friends, and enjoy our wide variety of meals. Located at the heart of Capitol Hill, we are dedicated to feeding the needy as well as the needful.

We stay in business with our helpful volunteers and full-time staff. Come ask our manager to volunteer and help out in your community today
